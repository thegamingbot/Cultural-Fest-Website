ABOUT US


Welcome to SHADOW. But what purpose does the name SHADOW serve ? SHADOW is all about stepping out of your shadow and unveiling your hidden talents at abilities. At SHADOW language, colour, state, branch does not matter at all. What matters is your will to participate and if that is there then nobody in the world can stop you from having the best time of your life. At SHADOW, we have everything; from competitions and events to carefree game stalls to delicious food stalls to amazing pro shows and performances that will leave you enthralled and catching for your breath and the end of it. It is all in 1 deal where everyone from the south to the north to the east and the west comes together in an extravagant exchange of Culture, Values, Ideals, Knowledge and most importantly Absolute Fun. So this is everything about us and if you want to know more, joinus at IIITDM Kancheepuram for SHADOW.
