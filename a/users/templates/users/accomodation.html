Accomodation


For the convenience of the enthusiastic participants of SHADOW, we here at IIITDM Kancheepuram take it upon ourselves to provide you with a convenient stay place within the institute itself.

Students will be placed in 3 hostels namely ASHWATHA, ASHOKA and JASMINE. Boys will be given rooms in ASHWATHA and ASHOKA while girls will stay in JASMINE.



















Due to the large number of requests for accommodation, we are bound to provide the rooms on a shared and first come first serve basis.

Availing the option to stay inside the institute shall come with direct entry to concerts, direct entry to pro shows, attending other events and competitions.

For any queries, you can email to shadow.culturalfest@gmail.com.
